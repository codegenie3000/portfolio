About me

I love building things and solving problems. It doesn’t matter whether it’s software, an interesting product, a company, or an organization.

Although I have a background in management consulting, I have always had a foot firmly rooted in technology. I started my career working within turnaround consulting where I helped companies succeed through adversity due to new competition, changes in the market, suboptimal management, or an unforeseen disaster. Working in turnaround consulting was immensely rewarding because it presented massive challenges which demanded creativity and resourcefulness.

Building on my experience with business and technology, I later co-founded Classroom Genie and I was responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining our web application in addition to strategic management. This experience was revelatory as it rekindled my passion for software and systems. Since starting this company, I have lived and breathed software development.

Projects

Classroom Genie

I co-founded Classroom Genie which helps K-12 teachers crowdfund to get Gift Cards from Amazon.com and Walmart so they can get more supplies for their classroom.

Classroom Genie Web App

The web application was built in MeteorJS and utilizes MongoDB. For its front end, it uses Bootstrap and Spacebars (a derivative of Handlebars). Functionality includes:

  • Automated creation of a user's crowdfunding campaign
  • Payment processing
  • Promo code management
  • Email notifications and marketing using Foundation Emails
  • Cron jobs

It also includes various NPM API integrations including Stripe and Mailgun.
